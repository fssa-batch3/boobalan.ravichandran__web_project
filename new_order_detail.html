About Product

Rilon Insecticide is a highly potent insecticide for use on lepidopterian Insects, which are considered the most damaging insects after sucking pests in edible crops such as vegetables and fruits. Rilon insecticide (Emamectin Benzoate 5% SG) has a unique mechanism. When applied, it absorbs through leaves (Trans-laminar) and forms a reservoir of molecules in each leaf. When targeting insect feed on the foliage, then Rilon Insecticide (Emamectin benzoate) molecule in leaves is released and inhibits muscle contraction and death of the insect.
